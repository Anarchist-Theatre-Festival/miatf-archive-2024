The 16th annual MONTREAL INTERNATIONAL ANARCHIST THEATRE FESTIVAL
May 23rd to May 25th 2023 with troupes from Belgium, France & Montreal

The annual Montreal International Anarchist Theatre Festival (MIATF) – the world’s biggest anarchist theatre festival – celebrates its 16th edition presenting five troupes from France, Belgium and Montreal for two nights of provocative, socially engaged, freedom-loving theatre, Tues May 23rd to Wed May 24th @ La Sala Rossa, 4848 boul St-Laurent, 7pm. There is also a benefit music show for the MITAF at the Casa del Popolo, Thursday May 25th at 8pm.

Tickets: $20 at the doors or online in advance (with a special two-night pass for $35)
No reserved seats. Doors open 7pm. Shows 7:30pm. Tickets:
https://casadelpopolo.com/en/events/la-sala-rossa

https://lepointdevente.com/tickets/czz230522002

Tuesday May 23
• Ji Kasi Apki Marzi, by Nitu Sharma (Montreal)
• Marusya Nikiforova: Ukraine’s legendary anarchist warrior, by Norman Nawrocki/Babushka Theatre (Montreal)
• CÍCLICAS: Resistiendo en la (de) (re) construcción, by Colectiva Tribu (Montreal)

Wednesday May 24
• J’accuze…! D’après l’affaire Dreyfus, by Le Krizo Théâtre (France)
• Le Contr’un, by Charly Magonza (Belgium)

Thursday May 25
A benefit show for the MIATF at the Casa del Popolo with:
• Kon-Fusion (Montreal)
• Tempolotov (Sherbrooke)
